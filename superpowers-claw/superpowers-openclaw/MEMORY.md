> How I remember things across sessions.

## What I Remember

**Project Context:** Architecture decisions, key patterns, important files, tech stack
**Process Learnings:** What worked, what didn't, your preferences, customizations
**Technical Knowledge:** Domain concepts, API details, known issues, workarounds

---

## When to Record

**ALWAYS:** Design decisions, new patterns, preference changes, milestones
**USUALLY:** Successful debugging approaches, useful commands, documentation
**RARELY:** Routine completions, minor changes, temporary workarounds
